One Day Package

Welcome to the One Day Package at Taj Hotel Bengaluru! This package is perfect for those who want to experience the best of Bengaluru in just one day.

Itinerary

Our expertly guided tour will take you to the following attractions:

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Visit Cubbon Park: Enjoy a peaceful stroll amidst lush greenery and stunning floral displays.
  • Lunch at a local eatery (not included)
  • Explore Bangalore Palace: Discover the grandeur and history of this iconic landmark, reminiscent of the city's royal past.
  • Shopping at MG Road: Browse through a variety of shops offering everything from clothing to souvenirs.
  • Dinner at the hotel (not included)

Our transportation services will be at your disposal throughout the day, so you can relax and enjoy the sights without worrying about logistics.

After a day of exploration, you will return to the hotel for a comfortable stay in our luxurious rooms. Our rooms are equipped with all the modern amenities to ensure a relaxing and comfortable stay. You will be staying in a Deluxe Room, which offers a comfortable king-sized bed, a spacious bathroom, and a beautiful view of the city.

Price

The price for the One Day Package is Rs.6,500 per person(Discounted Price)

Original Price if there is no group of students involved is Rs.10,000 per person.
